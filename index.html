06 — Semantic Colors: Danger, Warning, Success

Pure #FF0000, #FFCC00, and #00CC44 fight with branded surfaces because their hue sits at 0°, 50°, and 140° regardless of your palette's temperature. The fix: nudge each semantic hue ±15–25° toward your brand's hue, then match their lightness and saturation profile to the rest of your token system. They stay immediately readable as danger/warning/success while feeling like they belong.

07 — Pure Neutral vs Tinted Neutral

✗ Pure Neutral — disconnected

The brand color floats on top of grey. It reads as a guest in its own house. No relationship to the surface beneath it.

✓ Tinted Neutral — cohesive

The brand color intensifies what's already running through the page. It feels like it belongs. The background leans into your hue.
